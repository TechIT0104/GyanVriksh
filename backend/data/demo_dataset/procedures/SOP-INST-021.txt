FIELD INSTRUMENT CALIBRATION PROCEDURE
Applicable: Flow, pressure, level, temperature transmitters | Regulatory Basis: OISD-117
1. Obtain instrument work permit; inform panel officer before taking instrument out of service.
2. Record as-found readings at 0/25/50/75/100% against master calibrator.
3. Acceptance: within ±0.5% of span. Outside limits: adjust and record as-left values.
4. Flow meters: verify against master meter where line configuration allows.
5. Update calibration sticker and CMMS record. Deviations beyond ±5% must be reported
   to process engineering for historical data correction assessment.
