BHARAT CHEMICALS LIMITED — UNIT 3
SOP-EM-001 Rev 7 | Effective: 01-Nov-2025
EMERGENCY RESPONSE — HYDROCARBON LEAK
Regulatory Basis: OISD-141, Factories Act 1948
1. Raise alarm via nearest manual call point; inform shift supervisor on channel 1.
2. Isolate leak source remotely if safe (remote operated valves from control room).
3. Cordon area 15 m radius minimum; eliminate ignition sources; deploy water curtain if vapour cloud.
4. Leak from pump seal: switch to standby pump, isolate leaking pump, depressurise to flare.
5. HSE officer performs LEL survey before declaring area safe.
6. All leaks above 10 litres logged as incident with root cause analysis within 7 days.
