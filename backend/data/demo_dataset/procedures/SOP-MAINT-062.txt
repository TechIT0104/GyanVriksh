RECIPROCATING COMPRESSOR VALVE MAINTENANCE (K-101)
Regulatory Basis: OISD-116 Section 5
1. PTW with electrical isolation verified; barring gear engaged.
2. Depressurise and purge cylinder with nitrogen; verify LEL below 5% locally.
3. Remove valve covers; extract suction/discharge valve assemblies with OEM puller.
4. Inspect valve plates and springs; replace if any crack or wear step visible.
5. Torque valve cover bolts per OEM table; leak test at 5 bar nitrogen before handback.
6. Rod drop reading before and after maintenance; record in CMMS.
