BHARAT CHEMICALS LIMITED — UNIT 3
SOP-PTW-003 Rev 6 | Effective: 01-Jan-2025
PERMIT TO WORK SYSTEM
Regulatory Basis: OISD-145
1. Permit types: Cold Work, Hot Work, Confined Space Entry, Electrical, Excavation, Working at Height.
2. Hot work and confined space permits must be countersigned by a qualified HSE officer (minimum Grade-B).
3. Permit validity: one shift. Revalidation requires fresh gas test where applicable.
4. Permit must reference the applicable SOP and JSA number.
5. Site inspection by permit issuer before authorization is mandatory.
6. All permits closed out with site restoration check.
