CONFINED SPACE ENTRY PROCEDURE
Applicable: All vessels, columns, drums, tanks | Risk Level: HIGH
Regulatory Basis: OISD-105, Factories Act 1948 Section 36

1. Confined space entry permit (PTW-CS type) must be issued and countersigned by a
   Grade-B HSE officer before entry.
2. Section 6.2 — GAS TESTING: Oxygen 19.5-23.5%, LEL below 5%, H2S below 10 ppm, CO below 25 ppm.
   Gas testing must be performed not more than 30 minutes before entry. If entry is
   interrupted for more than 15 minutes, gas testing must be repeated before re-entry.
3. Section 6.3 — Continuous gas monitoring is mandatory for vessels with sludge or scale history.
4. Trained attendant (hole watch) must remain at the manway for the full duration of entry.
5. Rescue tripod, harness and SCBA set staged at the entry point.
6. Communication check every 15 minutes between entrant and attendant.
7. Entry log must record every entry and exit with timestamps.
