[Knowledge Capsule recording transcript — Ramesh Kumar, Senior Maintenance Technician, 22 years]
[Recorded for equipment: P-101, FI-101]

Dekho, P-101 ka jo seal hai na, woh kabhi achanak fail nahi hota. Pehle se bataata hai.
Ek halki si vibration aati hai, matlab bearing housing pe haath rakho toh feel hogi —
gauge pe shayad 4 ke aas paas dikhega, but feel alag hai. Aur ek halki whistling sound
aati hai suction side se. Jab yeh dono cheezein ek saath ho, samajh jao seal 10-15 din
mein jaayega. Main teen baar aise hi pakda hoon pehle se.

Aur subah startup se pehle, suction strainer zaroor check karo. Raat bhar mein sediment
settle hota hai, strainer chok ho jaata hai. Strainer choked matlab NPSH kam, matlab
cavitation, matlab seal pe stress. Yeh morning check koi SOP mein nahi likha proper se,
but yeh karna zaroori hai.

Ek aur baat — FI-101 flow meter. Woh hamesha 8 percent kam dikhata hai. Calibration ke
baad theek ho jaata hai, phir 6 mahine mein wapas drift. Orifice plate ka edge ghis gaya
hai. Toh jab bhi FI-101 ka reading dekho, 8 percent add kar lo mann mein. Venkatesan ko
bhi pata hai yeh baat.

Aur haan, agar P-101 mein cavitation ki awaaz aaye — jaise bajri ghum rahi ho pump mein —
toh pehle V-201 ka level dekho. Level 40 se neeche hai toh wahi problem hai. Level theek
hai toh suction line mein air pocket hoga, high point vent se nikaalo.
